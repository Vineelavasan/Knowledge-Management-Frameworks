# Knowledge-Management-Frameworks
Welcome to the Knowledge Management Frameworks repository. This repository provides an overview of widely used knowledge management (KM) frameworks, methodologies, and best practices that help organizations capture, organize, share, and utilize knowledge effectively.

The content is designed to support professionals involved in knowledge management, technical documentation, content strategy, organizational learning, digital transformation, and process improvement. It serves as a reference for understanding how knowledge flows within organizations and how structured frameworks can improve collaboration, decision-making, innovation, and operational efficiency.

The purpose of this repository is to:
- Provide practical knowledge management resources and examples.
- Explore established KM frameworks and methodologies.
- Support organizations in developing effective knowledge-sharing cultures.
- Improve knowledge discovery, accessibility, and reuse.
- Help teams implement scalable knowledge management strategies.

This repository may include:
- Knowledge Management Frameworks and Models
- Knowledge Capture and Retention Strategies
- Knowledge Sharing and Collaboration Practices
- Communities of Practice (CoP)
- Knowledge Governance Models
- Taxonomy and Information Architecture Examples
- Lessons Learned and Knowledge Repositories
- Organizational Learning Frameworks
- Knowledge Management Maturity Models
- Best Practices and Case Studies
